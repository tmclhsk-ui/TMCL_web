What We Do

Research Areas

Our research spans analytical modeling, experimental investigation, and intelligent control of thermal energy systems.

01 · HEAT PUMP
Water-Source Heat Pump Systems
Experimental and numerical investigation of water-source heat pump systems, including performance evaluation and optimization strategies under various operating conditions.
02 · VEHICLE
Vehicle Integrated Thermal Management
1-D transient modeling of electric vehicle thermal management systems and optimization of control logic for batteries, motors, and cabin climate.
03 · DIGITAL TWIN
Digital Twin via 1-D Transient Analysis
Developing digital twin environments using 1-D transient modeling on the Dymola platform to simulate and predict system behavior under various operating conditions.
04 · CONTROL
Model Predictive Control
Designing advanced MPC strategies for thermal systems to improve efficiency, responsiveness, and energy consumption in complex multi-component environments.
05 · HX DESIGN
Heat Exchanger Design
Research on design, performance characterization, and optimization of various heat exchangers including plate-type and falling-film evaporators for thermal systems.
06 · EXPERIMENT
System Performance Testing
Experimental measurement of heat pump system performance under various operating conditions including chemisorption systems, refrigerant mixtures, and pool boiling studies.
